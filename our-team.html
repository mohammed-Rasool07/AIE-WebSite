About Mr. Salman Ahmed

Er. Salman Ahmed

DCE.,B.E.,M.E.,(PhD)

Chairman & MD

Prof. Salman Ahmed is a well-known name in the engineering education circles of Hyderabad. He did his Diploma from Quli Qutub Shah Government Polytechnic, Hyderabad and went on to do his graduation from The College of Engineering, Hyderabad. He further pursued his Masters's Degree (M.E) from Osmania University.

Prof. Ahmed's journey in the teaching profession began in the year 2015 when he established Ahmed's Institute in Hyderabad. In a short span of time, the institute has become a well-known name in the city. He has motivated and guided thousands of engineering graduates for various competitive exams, and has been instrumental in producing top rankers.

He established Ahmed's Institute in 2015 which has successfully helped thousands of engineering students prepare for competitive exams. He is known for his lectures which are very inspiring and motivating. He has helped many students achieve their dreams of becoming top rankers in their exams.
